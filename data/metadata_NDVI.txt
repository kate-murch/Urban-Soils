Vegetation Cover (NDVI) – City of Calgary
- File Name: ndvi_points.csv
CSV (Comma Separated Values)
NAD84 / UTM Zone 11N 

This dataset contains vegetation cover values for Calgary, expressed as
Normalized Difference Vegetation Index (NDVI). This represents vegetation density ranging from 0 (bare or
impervious surface) to 1 (healthy vegetation).

City of Calgary Open Data Portal

Processing Steps:
1. The raster was loaded into QGIS.
2. The “Raster pixels to points” tool was used.
3. The resulting point layer was exported as a CSV file.

The prupose is to provide a dataset of vegetation cover within the City of Calgary
