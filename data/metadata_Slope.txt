Dataset Title: Digital Elevation Model (DEM) – City of Calgary
File Name: slope_degree.csv
Format: CSV (Comma Separated Values)
Coordinate Reference System (CRS): NAD83 / UTM Zone 11N (EPSG:26911)

Description:
Elevation data sampled from the City of Calgary’s Digital Elevation Model (DEM).
Values represent ground elevation in metres at each sampled point across the city.

Source:
City of Calgary Open Data Portal – “Digital Elevation Model (DEM)”
https://www.calgary.ca/maps/digital-elevation-model.html

Date Accessed: October 2025

Processing Steps:
1. Loaded DEM raster in QGIS.
2. Used the “Raster pixels to points” tool to convert cells to points.
3. Exported points as a CSV file.

Purpose:
To provide elevation values used in slope/erosion risk and Urban Soil Sustainability Index (USSI) analysis.
